No. Rules
1. All the CRs (class representatives) are advised to schedule assessments of other courses such that there is no clash with the above tentative schedule.
2. In case you are not able to give exam or any other assessment, remember that the assessment will not be re-taken at any cost.
3. Bring the lab manual in soft form. Mobile or smart phones are not allowed to view the manual. Your marks will be deducted otherwise.
4. Lab Manual and lab/theory slides are always in the process of being updated. Therefore, download the latest manual / slides using the link given above before coming to the lab every week. Don’t print out the whole lab manual.
5. Place the chairs in their proper place before leaving the lab/classroom. Turn off the lab computer, fans and lights before you leave the lab. Don’t write on the tables. Don’t throw away wrappers etc inside the lab. Your ethics will also be graded like your punctuality, attitude, discipline etc.
